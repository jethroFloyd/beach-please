Beach Please is a unique experiential 2 day music concert, where we recreate a real-life beach in an indoor environment. Bringing in some of the best electronic musical talents of the country, this event is a unique adventure in the sand where alongside the rave-party themed music, there will be fun beach activities, great food, a relaxed atmosphere and a one-of-a-kind experience!

Team

Graduates from the Cambridge School of Art and ex-Theatre Professors at the State University of New York, Kheya and Aditya from DhikChik LLP are accomplished performers. Aditya is a film-maker, actor, playwright and curator. Kheya has acted in over 10 feature films and has been an art and creative director for films and commercials. Together, they have curated India's largest mixed-media art installation project at the highly acclaimed Tram Tales; helped organize the Kolkata Literary Fest 2017 and produced and directed theatres at the State University of New York and films for BBC 4.
