Beach Please is a weekend extravaganza where we build an indoor beach for you. Chill with a drink at the shack, bury yourself in the white sand, light a bonfire, get psyched at the laser show or make sand castles: while some of the best artists in India play host for two raving nights.

The Lifeguards

Aditya is a filmmaker, writer, dramatist and film buff. Basically he's like every 3rd Bengali you'd find.
Kheya is an interdisciplinary artist, curator and creative arts director. She enjoys memes as well.
Graduates from the Cambridge School of Arts, they decided that their best bid to fame was to host beach parties. They explored different things on the way: they curated and created India's largest and one-of-a-kind art installation project at Tram Tales, helped organize the Apeejay Kolkata Literary Fest 2017, created, directed and acted in films for BBC 4 (among multiple others around the country), and directed plays at the State University of New York.
